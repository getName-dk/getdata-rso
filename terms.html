Private Local Administrator Tool

getData Remaster is a private, non-commercial local administrator tool for a small League of Legends custom game group. It is not distributed as an end-user application and is not operated as a public website or public leaderboard.

Telegram Bot Output

The getData operator may use a private Telegram bot to display generated match summaries, rankings, MVP/ACE records, and other statistics to authorized bot users and configured private group chats.

The service is not used for gambling, betting, esports wagering, prize distribution, or public competitive rankings.

Riot Account Linking
