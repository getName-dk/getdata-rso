Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-14

Overview

getData Remaster is a private local administrator tool for managing League of Legends custom game statistics for a small friend group. It is not distributed as an end-user application. Participants may voluntarily authorize Riot Sign On so the getData operator can access approved Riot account and match data for private local record management.

Data We May Collect After User Opt-In

  • Riot account identifiers such as PUUID, gameName, and tagLine.
  • League of Legends custom match identifiers and match statistics needed for private custom game summaries.
  • Internal getData player mapping information.
  • RSO access/refresh tokens if Riot Sign On is approved and implemented. Tokens are treated as private credentials.

How Data Is Used

  • To identify linked custom game participants.
  • To create migration candidates between existing local getData records and Riot match records.
  • To locally register or enrich private getData match records after administrator review.
  • To generate private MVP, ACE, ranking, performance score, session summary, and monthly summary features.
  • To display generated private summaries through a Telegram bot to authorized bot users and configured private group chats.

Data Sharing

Data is not sold, published, or exposed publicly. Generated summaries are shared only inside the private getData group through the Telegram bot and configured private group chats. The service is not used for gambling, betting, or esports wagering.

Account Disconnect and Deletion

Users can request disconnecting their Riot account and deletion of stored Riot account mappings or tokens at any time.

Security

API keys and tokens are stored privately and are never distributed to end users. Demo files do not include real keys, tokens, or private match data.

Contact

This project is operated privately by the getData administrator. Contact information can be provided in the Riot Developer Portal application.
